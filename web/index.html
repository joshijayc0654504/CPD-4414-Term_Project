Welcome to Click 'N' Shop

Click 'N' Shop is a manufacturer of widest range of ball & roller bearings and its components in India without any foreign collaboration, located at Junagadh in Gujarat State. Founded by four technocrats as a partnership firm in 1981, today the Company has grown with full array of high quality and precision bearings for various applications. Rapid development and flexible manufacturing system along with effective global delivery network enables us to deliver quality products with shorter lead time and competitive prices.
